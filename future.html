Future Steps


Summary

As we continue to grow and learn there are some areas where 4You would like to focus more of its time this includes: community involvement, customer support, distribution, market research, and media planning. We currently want to become more active within our communtiy by encoruaging customers to DM us as well as post about their flower experience. We even want them to be able to vote on how often they think we should have flower delivery launches. In the future, we also want to build out our customer support so that we can get back to customers in less than 24 hours for when they have questions or suggestings. The final thing that is key to the future of 4You thriving is building out our distribution channels. As we grow we will need more hands to help with delivering the product as well as finding a reliable manufacturer.
