About
4U is a flower delivery company with a focus on making flowers personal.
What is 4U?
4U is a flower delivery company with a focus on making flowers personal. See what the flowers will look like before they’re sent so the only surprise is that warm feeling of getting flowers!
Why should I donate through 4U?
We want to help Ukraine in the ways we know best, by directing our sales towards nonprofits that we believe in. Customers are also encouraged to donate directly to nonprofits that support Ukraine. Some of our favorites include Team Rubicon, Care, and Doctors Without Borders.
How do I know that my money is donated?
We encourage all customers to follow us on our Instagram (@4you.flowers). We will be posting proof of our donations following March 28th.
Can my flowers be delivered?
At this time, we do not offer direct deliveries to recipient addresses. Customer’s are invited to pickup their order at one of our designated pickup locations.
How is 4U different from other flower delivery services?
We are a student-run company based at USC’s Iovine and Young Academy. During regular drops, we allow customers to include a personalized handwritten messages within the packaging of their flower. Their message will be reproduced, in the customer’s original, handwritten font.
